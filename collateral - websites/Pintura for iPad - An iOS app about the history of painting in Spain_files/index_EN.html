Welcome to Pintura

The app Pintura helps the reader understand the evolution of painting in Spain, from prehistoric cave painting to 20th century surrealism. The content of Painting is suitable for children, students, and any person interested in gaining a deeper knowledge of Spain’s rich artistic history.


From cave painting, to surrealism

The artistic narrative was meticulously put together by a professor of art history with more than thirty years of experience. It includes the most representative movements and periods of Spanish art, such as cave painting, the Romanesque, the Gothic, the Renaissance, Baroque, Surrealism, and others. Every artistic movement is accompanied by an accessible introduction to the political and social context that influenced its members.

Amazing user experience

The app is 100% native for Apple iOS, and it was developed under strict optimal user experience criteria. High resolution image paintings, including Retina Display compatibility for the majority of content. The content is accessible without Internet connection. Apple Education Volume Purchasing Discount available for academic institutions.

Comprehensive and interactive

More than 100 pages of original text were written exclusively for this app. It contains more than 10 interactive works, allowing a large range of zoom levels, and specific commentary for relevant zones of the painting.
